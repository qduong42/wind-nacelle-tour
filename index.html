    What actually lives inside a wind turbine nacelle?

    You monitor gearbox oil temperatures and generator windings every day — here is the machine those signals come from, laid out as an interactive cutaway.

    • Orbit & click — drag to orbit, click any numbered hotspot to fly to a component
    • Watch it run — the drivetrain spins with a real ~1:100 gear ratio
    • X-Ray — see through the nacelle shell; with a component selected, it opens that component’s own casing instead
    • Two machines — toggle (top right) between the geared turbine and an Enercon-style direct drive
